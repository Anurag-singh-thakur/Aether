1. Introduction

 1.1 Project Overview

  The project is mainly based on an eye tracking system that tracks how much time a student has spent on reading a study material given by the teacher and then represents this data to the teacher in the form of a bar chart.

 1.2 Project Scope

  Other than providing a platform for the teachers and students to communicate, the main objective of this system is to provide a way for the teacher to better evaluate his/her students in a more efficient way.
